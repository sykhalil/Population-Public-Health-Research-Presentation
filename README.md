# 📱 mHealth Interventions for Prediabetes

## 📌 Overview
This project explores the role of mobile health (mHealth) interventions in supporting lifestyle and behavioral changes among adults with prediabetes.

---

## ❓ Research Question
> How effective are mHealth-based interventions in promoting lifestyle and behavioral changes among adults with prediabetes, and what barriers impact their adoption and long-term use?

---

## 🧪 Methodology
This project is based on a **literature review and synthesis** of existing research.

- Reviewed studies on:
  - 📱 Mobile applications  
  - 💬 Text-message interventions  
  - 🌐 Web-based programs  
- Evaluated:
  - Behavioral outcomes (diet, physical activity)  
  - Clinical outcomes (weight loss, A1c reduction)  
- Identified key challenges:
  - Engagement and adherence  
  - Accessibility and digital divide  
  - Privacy and security concerns  

---

## 📊 Findings

### ✅ Effectiveness
- Improved adherence to lifestyle interventions  
- Supported weight loss and glycemic control  
- Enabled continuous monitoring via PGHD  
- Increased access to care  
- Scalable and cost-effective solutions  

### ⚙️ Key Features
- Goal setting and feedback  
- Activity and nutrition tracking  
- Remote coaching and support  
- Motivational reminders  

### ⚠️ Barriers
- Low long-term engagement (app fatigue)  
- Privacy and data security concerns  
- Limited access to devices and internet  
- Digital literacy challenges  
- Limited personalization
